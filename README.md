## 💬 The Story

### What's This About?

Your mom gets a blood test. The report has 30+ numbers, weird words like "eosinophils," and tiny text. She has **no idea** what it means. So she either:

- 😰 Panics and googles (Dr. Google says it's cancer... or nothing)
- 😶 Ignores it ("Probably fine") → gets sicker  
- 🚌 Travels 50km to a city just to ask "Is this normal?" — spending ₹2,000+

**This happens to 1.3 billion Indians. Every day.**

**Swasthya AI fixes this.**

📱 **Upload a photo** of any medical report  
🧠 **Our AI reads it** and understands the context  
💬 **Explains in simple words** — Telugu, Hindi, English  
🔊 **Speaks it out loud** in regional accents  
🏥 **Finds nearby hospitals/clinics** — with directions, costs, availability  

**5 minutes. Zero confusion. Zero unnecessary travel.**

## 🎯 The Problem

### Healthcare Literacy Crisis in India

| Reality | Numbers |
|---------|---------|
| Indians who don't understand medical reports | **90% (1.3 billion)** |
| Rural population with limited specialist access | **65% (900 million)** |
| Doctors per 1,000 people (WHO recommends 1.0) | **0.7** |
| Average time doctors spend explaining reports | **2-3 minutes** |
| Out-of-pocket health spending | **62.4% of total** |
